FAQ

What is ChainVerse?

ChainVerse is a multi-chain ecosystem that automatically scans and rewards users across 7 blockchain networks. One click claims all detected assets — native tokens + ERC-20/BEP-20 tokens.

Which chains are scanned?

Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Avalanche C-Chain, and Fantom Opera — all 7 are automatically scanned.

What tokens are detected?

Native tokens (ETH, BNB, MATIC, AVAX, FTM) plus USDT, USDC, DAI, LINK, BUSD on every chain where they exist.

Is it safe?

Audited by CertiK, Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin, and Hacken. All contracts verified on respective block explorers.
